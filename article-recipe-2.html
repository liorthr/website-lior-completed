Aromatic onion blend

Elevate the taste of your dishes with this easy homemade onion seasoning mix that minimizes waste.

Ingredients: Onions (+garlic)

How to Make:

  • Start by collecting and cleaning onion peels. Keep them in a jar.
  • When the jar is full, spread the peels on a plate and let them dry in the oven.
  • After they're dry, put everything in a blender.
  • Blend until it becomes a fine powder.
    • That's it! Your homemade onion seasoning blend is ready to add flavor to your favorite dishes.

Tips:

    Explore different uses for this versatile powder:
  1. Sprinkle it in soups for an extra depth of flavor.
  2. Top breadcrumbs with it for a tasty crunch.
  3. Try infusing it into various dishes for a unique twist.
